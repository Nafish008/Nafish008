	<u><Strong>Harry Potter</Strong></u><!-- <u> is a underline tag --> is a series of fantasy novels written by Britishauthor J. K. Rowling.<br><!-- <br> is a next line tag --> 
	The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends <u><b>Hermione Granger</b><!-- <b> is a bold tag --></u> and 
	<u><b>Ron Weasley</b></u>, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story 
	arc concerns Harry's struggle against <u><b>Lord Voldemort</b></u>, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, 
	overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people).<br>
